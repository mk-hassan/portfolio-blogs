- About 2 minutes task, you define core entities that your apis use and the system will persist in a Data Model.

- Write it in shape of bullet list and **Don't forget** to tell the interviewer that this list is a draft and will be updated as we go.

- Don't list the entire data model (columns and fields of the entities) this will be done on the High-level design phase. 
  As you design your system you will discover new entities and relationships you didn't discover before.

- Start simple and update as you go

- Aim to choose good names for your entities
